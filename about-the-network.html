About the Network

The Thames Valley & Wessex Neonatal Operational Delivery Network’s philosophy is ‘to give the highest possible standard of safe, effective care to babies and their families.

We encourage all the units in the Network to work in partnership so that these babies can be cared for by the appropriate staff in the most appropriate unit as close to home as possible. Every member of staff within every unit will try their utmost to give the optimal care for your baby and family.

Neonatal intensive care is a very specialised service, and sometimes it may be necessary to transfer your baby to another unit to allow another baby to receive intensive care.

All the staff throughout the Network have collaborated on the information in this app to give parents as much information as possible about the individual units.

We would be very grateful for any feedback that you could give us about how we could improve our services for future babies and families.
