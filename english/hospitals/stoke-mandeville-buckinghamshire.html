Visiting information

Parents
Free to visit any time day or night

Number of visitors
No more than 2 visitors to each cot (including one parent)

Siblings
can visit from 14:30hrs -19:00hrs

Restrictions
visitors other than parents can only visit from 17:00hrs-19:00hrs. No children under the age of 18 can visit unless they are the babies siblings.

Grandparents
can visit between 17:00hrs and 19:00hrs – 2 visitors per cot including one parent

Hygiene and infection prevention
all visitors must wash their hands and apply alcohol gel on entering the unit. No outdoor clothing permitted on the unit . No food or hot drinks can be consumed in the nurseries.

Family and friends
can visit between 17:00hrs and 19:00hrs – 2 visitors per cot including one parent

What to expect when you arrive

How to access the Unit
The unit is located in the Claydon Wing, Access is via an intercom system for your baby’s safety.

Talking to the medical team
Please ask the nurse allocated to your baby to contact the doctor if you need an update and are unable to attend the ward round.

Who to talk to about your baby’s care
Any member of staff will be happy to help you with any queries. Your baby will be allocated a nurse at the beginning of every shift and they will keep you updated with regard to babies care.

Ward round
every morning from approximately 09:00hrs. you are welcome to be present during the ward round but occasionally may be asked to leave when the Dr’s are discussing other patients.

Who to talk to if you have any concerns
Any member of the neonatal team will be happy to assist you with any concerns or queries.

Baby rest time
12:30hrs -14:30hrs. Whenever possible we dim the lights and minimally handle your baby avoiding carrying out any non-urgent procedures during this time.
