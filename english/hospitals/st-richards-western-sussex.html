Cashpoint machine
A cash machine is located in main reception next to Friends Shop.

Religious and spiritual support
We are able to accommodate the need of all faiths. Chaplin’s of main denominations are happy to visit the ward and meet parents or relatives. Ward staff can arrange this.

Catering/refreshment facilities
The Terrace Restaurant is located on the first floor of the main hospital and open daily from 8.00a.m. until 7.30 p.m.

Cloisters Café is located on the ground floor in main reception 9.30am - 4.30p.m.

WRVS Tea Bar located on ground floor outpatients department 9.30 a.m. – 5.00 p.m.

Patient Advocacy Liaison Service (PALS)
Patient Advice and Liaison Service (PALS) office is located at main reception and is open weekdays from 9.00a.m. – 5.00p.m.

Interpreting services
This can be arranged for an interpreter if necessary and this can be arranged through the ward.

Other shops on site/nearby
The Friends of Chichester Hospital Shop is located in main reception 9.30a.m. until 6.00 p.m.

Newsagent just outside the main entrance and main shops are 15mins walking

Access to a midwife
To see a midwife go to Tangmere Post-Natal Ward, 2nd Floor.

Laundry facilities
Hopsital Laundry services

By car

Parking available front of Women’s and Children’s Block (old Maternity) also parking adjacent to Main Hospital Entrance.

Car parking charges…

  • £1.60 up to 1 Hrs
  • £2.60 1 – 2 Hrs
  • £3.50 2 – 3 Hrs
  • £4.00 3 – 4 Hrs
  • £6.00 over 4 Hrs

Parents staying on the Neo – Natal Unit please check with nurse in charge with reference to charges.

By bus

St Richards Hospital is served by the number 55 Bus Service which runs between Chichester and Tangmere. Stagecoach South stops in Spitafield Lane outside the hospital.

By Train

Chichester Railway Station 20mins walk or around 10 minutes in Taxi or No 55 Bus.
